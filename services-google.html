Google Management

Your Google Page Is Your Most Valuable Asset. Is Anyone Managing It?

Your Google Business profile ranks above your website. It's the first thing new customers see. For most local businesses, it's completely unmanaged.

Why It Matters

Google Business matters more than your website.

In local search, your Google Business Profile appears before any organic results — sometimes before paid ads. It shows your hours, phone number, reviews, and photos before a single person clicks.

That means most customers make a decision about your business before they ever visit your site.

76% of people who search for a local business on their phone visit that business within 24 hours. Your Google profile is the deciding factor.

It's free visibility — but only if it's optimized and actively maintained. An abandoned Google page signals to customers and to Google that your business is stagnant.
