Local SEO

What local SEO actually means — in plain English

Local SEO means showing up when someone in your city searches for what you do. "Auto detailing Redding CA." "Chiropractor near me." "Best pest control Anderson."

When you rank in those searches, you get customers who are already looking for you — no ads required.

What we do: Page titles, meta descriptions, local keywords, schema markup, Google Search Console setup, and ongoing content optimization every month.
