Our Story

We built this because we lived the problem.

Sundial Systems was built because we know what it's like to run a local business in Northern California.

We built Vivid Detailz Auto Spa from the ground up in Redding. We know the missed calls. The slow seasons. The customers who come once and never hear from you again. The Google Business page you set up years ago and haven't touched since.

We built systems to fix all of that for ourselves — then started building them for other business owners who were dealing with the same things.

We work with contractors, chiropractors, auto shops, restaurants, and service businesses across Shasta County and the North State because we live here too. This isn't a remote agency pitching you on a Zoom. This is a conversation between neighbors.
